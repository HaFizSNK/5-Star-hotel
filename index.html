Radisson Blu hotel near Ordu city center on the Black Sea

This expansive Ordu hotel offers guests deluxe accommodation near the sparkling Black Sea, just 26 kilometers from Ordu-Giresun Airport (OGU). Guests can enjoy the area's beaches, rivers and lush mountains before returning to the hilltop hotel via an aerial lift, which also connects the hotel to the city center.


Wake up to sweeping views of Ordu and the Black Sea

Choose from 129 comfortable rooms and suites with floor-to-ceiling windows that permit beautiful views of the verdant landscape and sea. Featuring illuminated nature prints and decorated in rich neutral tones and muted turquoise, these elegant accommodations provide a soothing environment for a restful stay.

    Discover the nearby attractions in Ordu

    The village-like atmosphere of Boztepe puts local highlights within walking distance of our Ordu hotel. Just 200 meters away, guests can soar even higher than Boztepe's 550-meter elevation by parasailing. Sample Turkish dishes like roasted pickles, stuffed kale rolls, and Black Sea pita bread, at the local restaurants, pubs, and bars. Turkey's coast yields fresh seafood, such as the Black Sea anchovy, whiting, and turbot. You can also walk to souvenir shops and nut farms, where you can get a taste of Ordu's world-leading industry of hazelnut production. Ordu's beautiful beach is only 15 minutes away. For day trips, you can choose a visit to a seaside national park or tour local stone fortresses. Read more about what to visit with our suggestions below.
